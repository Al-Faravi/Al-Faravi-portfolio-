Research Paper

A Web-Based Project for Detecting Chest Diseases Using Deep Learning

Authors: MD. Shakawat Hossain Faravi, MD. Atik Hasan Emon, MD. Saklain Ahmed

Abstract

Chest X-ray classification serves as crucial for the early detection and handling of thoracic diseases. Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) are used in this project's web-based system to categorize chest X-ray images into six groups: Covid-19, Emphysema, Healthy, Pneumonia, Tuberculosis, and Random.

Key Methodology

  • Dataset: 12,000 photos obtained from Kaggle.
  • Model: ResNet101 (Outperformed DenseNet & EfficientNet).
  • Accuracy: Achieved 96.92% total accuracy.
